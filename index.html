About Us

The Good Hope Clinic is located in Nairobi, Kenya. Contruction started on September 2014 , completed on August the following year and officially opened on November. It was later renamed. it is located in the Upper Hill part of Nairobi Good Hope Clinic is dedicated to offering patients and their families, efficient service and great value for their money.

We are devoted to providing a warm, friendly and caring environment in which patients can recover. We are an organization that inspires our staff to achieve the extraordinary and develop the best careers.
